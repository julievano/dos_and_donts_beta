Working together

The Hydrologic Research and Applications Toolbox is being developed by a diverse team of scientists and engineers in the Hydrometeorological Applications Program at the National Center for Atmospheric Research in close collaboration with multiple management agencies and Universities across the Nation and around the world. Our overarching goal is to advance our understanding of the land surface and atmospheric processes to serve a range of hydrometeorological applications for the benefit of society.
