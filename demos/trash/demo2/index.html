Welcome to the Computational Hydrology Website

This site provides an integrated collection of tools to support future water resources planning and management. This includes advances in hydrometeorological datasets, climate downscaling, hydrologic modeling, climate impact assessements, and streamflow prediction, packaged as open-source software, freely available datasets, and guidance on appropriate use - collectively providing a foundation for understanding and adapting to future change.
